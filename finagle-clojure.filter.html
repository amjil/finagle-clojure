finagle-clojure.filter

Wrapper around com.twitter.finagle.Filter. Use and-then to compose Filters & Services together.

and-then

(and-then filter next)

Compose a Filter and Filter or Service together.

Arguments:

  • filter: a Filter.
  • next: a Filter or Service.

Returns:

A new Service that will first pass the request through filter and then pass through next.

See: chain for a higher level interface.

chain

(chain & filters-and-service)

Compose a series of Filters & Services together. This is shorthand for calling and-then multiple times.

Arguments:

  • filters-and-service: variadic argument, the Filters & Service you want to compose together.

Returns:

A new Service.

See: and-then.
